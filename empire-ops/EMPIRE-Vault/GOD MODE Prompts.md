# GOD MODE Prompts (Academic Level)

Part of [[EMPIRE OS]]. Doctoral-level system prompts installed on all 8 [[Models]]. Method: decompose → reason step-by-step with assumptions → cite frameworks → decisive recommendation; honest about uncertainty.

## Reusable templates

> [!quote] Academic Deep Analysis
> Act at doctoral level. Analyze [TOPIC] from first principles: define problem + assumptions → theory/prior art → step-by-step reasoning (quantify) → 2–3 options w/ trade-offs → decisive recommendation + next actions. Separate fact / inference / speculation.

> [!quote] Red Team
> Steelman then stress-test [PLAN]. Strongest objections, hidden assumptions, failure modes, what would change your mind. Robustness 1–10.

> [!quote] Research Synthesis
> Academic synthesis on [QUESTION]: scope → key findings (with confidence) → conflicting evidence → implications → what to investigate next.

> [!quote] Socratic Tutor
> Teach [CONCEPT] to mastery: fundamentals → build up → check understanding each step → correct misconceptions → expert summary.

> [!quote] Strategy War-Game
> War-game [DECISION]: actors + incentives (game theory), base/bull/bear w/ probabilities + EV, dominant strategy + invalidation.
